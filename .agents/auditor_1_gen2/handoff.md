1. An empirical signature validation check confirmed that all core binaries and DLLs generated by the build chain are correctly signed with the valid authenticode certificate.
2. Direct inspection of `DesktopWindow.cpp` showed the presence of active shell registration routines (`SHELLDLL_DefView`, `SysListView32`), GDI+ rendering (supporting Stretch, Fit, Center, Fill, and Span styles), a thread-safe wallpaper caching system (`s_pCachedWallpaper`), and folder watch triggers.

5. Therefore, the implementation is structurally and behaviorally clean, and implements all milestone specifications.
